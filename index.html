Originated in 1916 as Bayerische Flugzeug-Werke, a builder of aircraft engines, but assumed the name Bayerische Motoren Werke in July 1917 and began producing motorcycles in the 1920s. BMW entered the automobile business in 1928. The company's R32 motorcycle set a world speed record that was not broken until 1937.

Biography of BMW

BMW is a German car and motorcycle manufacturer founded in 1916. They are known for their luxury vehicles and motorcycles, combining performance and elegant design. BMW also embraces electric mobility and has a rich history in motorsports. Today, BMW is a global symbol of luxury and driving pleasure.
